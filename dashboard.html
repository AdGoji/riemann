You're clear for launch.

Riemann-dash is a Sinatra app that makes it easy to write complex views over thousands of states. It queries Riemann's index for the states you care about and presents them as charts. Hover over any state for details.

Get started at Github, or see the quickstart guide. It's available as a Gem.
