It's dangerous to go alone. Take this!

IRC Channel

Hop over to irc.freenode.net and join #riemann. I'm almost always on channel, and try to answer questions during my normal waking hours--typically 10:00 to 00:00, Pacific time. We've got quite a few Riemann users in channel who also pitch in to help answer questions. If you don't get a response right away, try to stay in channel--I catch up on the backlog and answer questions when I wake up.

If you're asking a new question, or have to leave without getting an answer, check the channel logs.

All kinds of useful stuff there!

Github Issues

Riemann Issues tracks outstanding bugs, and you can ask for help, suggest a feature, or report a problem there.

Google Group

The riemann-users mailing list is a good place to ask questions.

Twitter

You can mention @aphyr on twitter with feedback or questions.
